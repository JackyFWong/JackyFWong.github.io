Lockup

Background

I wanted to create a design for an app that was relatively simple but allowed me some flexibility in what I could actually design. Because I was studying for a few exams at the time of planning, I decided to create an application that would help me, or anybody else using it, study more effectively by staying away from distractions on your phone. Thus, introducing Lockup.

I started with a wireframe for what I want the functionality of Lockup to look like. After some refinement and additional screens, I had a good idea of what elements I needed.

I then worked on a mood board, basically getting inspiration as to what I want Lockup to look and feel like. All of these designs came from Dibbble.

I finally worked on and finished Lockup's final designs in Adobe XD. There was a bit more improvement as I went along, but it was not too bad.

I also created a working prototype by using InVision and overlaying functinality onto exported images of my final design.
